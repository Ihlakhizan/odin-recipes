Jeera Rice

Description

A very popular dish of fluffy basmati rice tempered with cumin seeds and fresh whole spices. Goes great with any curry, like our very own Rajma Masala!

Extremely simple recipe - anyone can make it!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup basmati rice
  • 4 cups water
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 2 green cardamoms
  • 1 black cardamom
  • 1/2 inch cinnamon
  • 2 cloves
  • 1 strand mace
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • 2 teaspoons cumin seeds
  • 1 green chilli, chopped
  • 1 tablespoon coriander leaves

Steps

  1. Rinse rice till water runs clear. Soak for 30 minutes and drain water, then set aside.
  2. Heat 4 cups water till boiling. Add whole spices, salt, and rice.
  3. Rice grains should be cooked till tender and yet separate. Set cooked rice aside.
  4. Heat oil in a small pan. Add cumin and let it crackle and brown.
  5. Add green chillies.
  6. Add the tempering to the hot rice.
  7. With a fork, gently mix with the rice grains.
  8. Add coriander leaves and mix.
  9. Serve hot.
