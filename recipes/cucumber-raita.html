Cucumber Raita

Description

A cool and refreshing savory condiment made with cucumber and curd!

Easy as pie, and twice as tasty!

Ingredients

  • 1 finely chopped medium-sized cucumber
  • 1 cup curd
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1 teaspoon roasted cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chaat masala powder
  • Salt as required
  • 1 tablespoon chopped coriander leaves

Steps

  1. Rinse, peel, and finely chop the cucumber.
  2. Whisk the curd until smooth.
  3. Add the cucumber to the curd.
  4. Add all the spice powders, salt as needed, and the chopped coriander leaves.
  5. Combine well and serve.
